# Global Sport Discovery Is Not Workspace-Scoped

Esporteam discovery and sport session participation are global across sport profiles: organizers host sessions, can join sessions hosted by others, and enthusiasts can discover any compatible public session. The workspace service is reserved for future administrative or operational boundaries and must not be used today as the tenant boundary for public discovery, session matching, invitations, or participation.

## Consequences

- Safety and authorization are enforced by sport profile ownership, session host permissions, visibility, blocks, reports, match rules, and internal capacity.
- Discovery cards may show total participant count, but must not expose remaining slots or whether capacity is full before a match/action succeeds.
- Platform subscriptions may unlock organizer or enthusiast capabilities, but they are not participant fees for sport sessions.
